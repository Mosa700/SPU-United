Who Are We?

Sol Plaatje University United (SPU utd) newly founded in 2022 by Sol Plaatje University (SPU) opened in 2014, the first of two new universities to be established in post-apartheid South Africa. It is also the first university to be established in the Northern Cape Province. In developing the focus for its academic disciplines, SPU has looked to the unique needs, competence, and characteristics of the Northern Cape region. This approach brought the current focus areas of teacher education, ICT and data science, heritage studies, paleo-sciences, and creative writing in African languages to the fore.
